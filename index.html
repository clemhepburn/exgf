Kaly Scheller-Barrett is the cloths magician and clothes horse behind EXGF. She’s a German immigrant and queer femme who lives with two sweet and stinky dogs in Oakland, California and just wants to be comfortable at all times.


Ex Girlfriend Alterations is on a quest to bring total embodiment and gender euphoria to the people. Currently run out of the Mutual Stores project space in Oakland, California, EXGF wants you to feel at home in your body and in your clothes. At EXGF, we believe that textile knowledge should be shared, that clothes should be repaired, and that the best garment is the one that already exists.
